Navigation Structure

  • Company Switcher — top of sidebar, switch between HOI / HOK / HOR / Group view. All pages filter accordingly.
  • Shared modules — Dashboard, Sales, Orders, Inventory, Products, Customers, Reports, Settings
  • Company-specific modules — Manufacturing (HOK only), Consulting (HOR only), Inter-Company Transfers
  • Role-based visibility — Staff see their company only. Managers see their company + reports. Admins see everything + Group view.
